Who We Are

Engineering Resilience
Through Experience

A Mumbai-based rubber compounding company built on over two decades of collective expertise across rubber compounding, raw material trading, and investment financing.

Rubix Rubber Ventures LLP is a Mumbai-based rubber compounding company built on a strong foundation of industry expertise and commercial acumen.

The firm brings together over two decades of collective experience across rubber compounding, raw material trading, and investment financing, enabling a well-rounded and practical understanding of both manufacturing and market dynamics.

Our vision is to deliver high-performance, engineered rubber compounds that consistently meet the exacting standards of industries where quality, reliability, and long-term performance are critical. We approach every requirement with a solution-oriented mindset, ensuring that each compound is tailored to specific application needs and operating conditions.

At the core of our operations is a strong focus on controlled, in-house manufacturing. By establishing a dedicated compounding facility, we maintain close oversight across the entire production lifecycle — from raw material selection and formulation to processing and quality validation. This integrated approach allows us to ensure consistency in every batch, reduce dependency on external variables, and respond with agility to evolving customer requirements.

Our technical and commercial experience enables us to bridge the gap between material science and real-world application. We work closely with customers to understand performance expectations, cost sensitivities, and end-use environments, allowing us to develop compounds that deliver both functional reliability and commercial efficiency.

Rubix Rubber serves a diverse range of industries, including automotive, electrical, oil & gas, and plumbing. Our solutions are designed to perform under demanding conditions, offering durability, precision, and consistency across applications.

As we continue to grow, our focus remains on building long-term partnerships through dependable quality, transparent business practices, and a commitment to continuous improvement. By combining experience-driven insight with modern manufacturing capabilities, we aim to be a trusted partner for engineered rubber solutions.
