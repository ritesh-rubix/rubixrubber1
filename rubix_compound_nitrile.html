Nitrile

Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) is the industry standard for oil and fuel resistance. Excellent resistance to petroleum oils, hydraulic fluids, and fuels, with good mechanical properties and abrasion resistance. Widely used for seals, O-rings, gaskets, and hoses in oil & gas, automotive, and industrial applications.
