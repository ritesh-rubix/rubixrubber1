EPR

Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPR/EPDM) offers excellent electrical insulation properties combined with outstanding resistance to heat, ozone, UV radiation, and weathering. Unlike general EPDM, EPR grades are specifically formulated for wire and cable applications with optimised electrical properties. Used in medium and high-voltage power cables, transformer insulation, and control cables operating in outdoor or wet environments.
